What gets removed?

Instagram adds these parameters to identify who shared the link. Removing them prevents the recipient from knowing who sent it.

?igsh=

Encodes your sharing session — used to track who shared the post.

?igshid=

Older version of the share ID tracker. Same privacy risk.

?utm_source=

Tells Instagram where the traffic came from (e.g. share sheet).

?utm_medium=

Identifies how the link was shared (DM, copy link, etc.).
