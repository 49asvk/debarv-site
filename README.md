# DebArv - A Modern Cute Couple Creative space

Welcome to our shared space for blogs, projects, and creative endeavors. Built with a modern, elegant aesthetic that celebrates Deb & Arv's partnership.

## ✨ Features

- **Beautiful Blog Platform**: Share your thoughts and ideas
- **Project Showcase**: Display your work and creations
- **Modern Design**: Clean, elegant, and romantic theme
- **Responsive**: Works perfectly on all devices

## 🚀 Getting Started

Visit [debarv.com](https://debarv.com) to explore our content.

### For Contributors

- Add blogs by editing `script.js` and adding images to `blogs/images/`
- Add projects by editing `script.js` and adding images to `projects/images/`
- See [STRUCTURE.md](STRUCTURE.md) for detailed organization
- See [DESIGN.md](DESIGN.md) for design customization

## 💝 Design Philosophy

Our site is designed with warmth and elegance, featuring a modern aesthetic with romantic touches. The color palette uses warm terracottas and soft rose tones to create a welcoming, intimate space.
